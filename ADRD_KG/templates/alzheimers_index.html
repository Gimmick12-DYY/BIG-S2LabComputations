AD Data Format Guide
Required Columns for AD Analysis:
  • Patient_ID: Unique patient identifier
  • Age: Patient age in years
  • Gender: Male/Female
  • MMSE_Score: Mini-Mental State Examination (0-30)
  • CDR_Score: Clinical Dementia Rating (0, 0.5, 1, 2, 3)
  • Clinical_Diagnosis: CN (Cognitively Normal), MCI, AD
Optional Biomarker Columns:
  • CSF_ABeta42: CSF Aβ42 levels (pg/mL)
  • CSF_Total_Tau: CSF total tau levels (pg/mL)
  • CSF_Phospho_Tau: CSF p-tau levels (pg/mL)
  • APOE_Genotype: APOE genotype (ε2/ε3, ε3/ε3, etc.)
